About Amber Glass Films

Amber Glass Films are high-performance window films designed to offer superior protection against harmful UV rays and blue light while maintaining a warm, visually appealing amber tint. Ideal for laboratories, healthcare environments, offices, and residential spaces, these films provide both functional and aesthetic benefits.

Crafted with advanced optical-grade materials, amber films help preserve sensitive materials, reduce glare, enhance privacy, and regulate interior temperatures. They are especially effective in environments where light-sensitive materials or processes are used, making them a popular choice in pharmaceutical and scientific settings.
